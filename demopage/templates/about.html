About Blood Group Identification System

Our Mission

Our blood group identification system aims to provide a quick and reliable way to determine blood types using advanced image processing and machine learning techniques. We strive to make blood type identification more accessible and efficient for healthcare professionals.

How It Works

Our system uses sophisticated image analysis to detect blood type reactions in standard blood typing tests. By uploading a clear image of a blood typing test card, our algorithm can:

  • Analyze agglutination patterns
  • Detect blood type markers
  • Determine ABO and Rh blood groups
  • Provide quick and accurate results

Benefits

  • Quick and accurate blood type identification
  • Reduced human error in interpretation
  • Easy-to-use interface
  • Accessible from any device
  • Secure data handling
